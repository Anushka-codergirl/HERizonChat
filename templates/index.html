HERizonChat

HERizonChat is your interactive chatbot assistant, providing quick answers and valuable insights on women's equality, empowerment, and relevant global topics like the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Whether you're seeking information on gender equality, laws affecting women, or global initiatives for women’s empowerment, HERizonChat is here to help. Ask anything about women’s rights, or SDG reports, or get inspired by the stories of women breaking barriers worldwide.

FAQs

To start a conversation, click on the chat icon at the bottom right of the page. Once the chat window opens, ask something, and click the send icon to get your answer.

HERizonChat is designed to answer any questions related to women's equality, empowerment, and SDG reports. You can ask about gender equality, rights, laws, statistics, and global initiatives supporting women. It also provides insights into how different countries are working towards achieving the SDGs that focus on women's welfare.

Currently, HERizonChat is available in English. Stay tuned for updates!
